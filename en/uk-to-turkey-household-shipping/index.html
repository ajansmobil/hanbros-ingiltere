Door-to-door moving

Menu menu

Hanbros UK plans residential moves as a closed chain—survey, packing crew, UK export formalities, main carriage and Turkish address handover. Ask early about restricted items, plants and high-value declarations so paperwork matches the physical load.

Typical milestones

  1. On-site or video survey and cubic estimate
  2. Packing dates aligned with tenancy or completion
  3. Loading, sealing and export documentation
  4. Sea or multimodal main leg to Turkey
  5. Import clearance support and final positioning in rooms

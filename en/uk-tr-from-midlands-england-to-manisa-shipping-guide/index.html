Corridor: Hanbros UK moves goods and relocations from the United Kingdom to Turkey (UK→TR). This guide is for consignments that start in the Midlands and are delivered toward Manisa.

UK collection (the Midlands)

Share postcodes, site access (lifts, low bridges, parking suspensions) and preferred loading windows. Commercial lines need clear HS codes and stackability notes; household moves need a room-by-room inventory for export packing.

UK export checklist

  • Commercial invoice and packing list aligned to the physical load.
  • GB EORI and Incoterms agreed; named importer of record in Turkey where required.
  • Insurance values that match CMR, marine or air waybill limits.
  • Restricted, dual-use and lithium battery items declared before booking.

Main carriage toward Manisa

Typical patterns combine a UK port or ro-ro feed (Felixstowe, London Gateway, Southampton, Liverpool, Tilbury) with a deep-sea or short-sea leg into Turkish hubs serving Manisa. Time-critical cargo may use air via UK gateways into IST/ADB/ESB subject to airline product and DG rules.

Arrival in Turkey

Prepare Turkish tax identifiers and customs representation for release in the Manisa catchment. Hanbros UK issues milestone updates through UK departure, main carriage and Turkish handover.

Hanbros UK on this domain only markets exports from the United Kingdom to Turkey—commercial products, retail freight and relocations. Request a written quote when you are ready to move stock or personal effects from the Midlands to Manisa.

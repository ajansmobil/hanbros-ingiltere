Corridor note: Hanbros UK on hanbroscargo.co.uk specialises United Kingdom to Turkey (UK→TR) door-to-door logistics. This guide answers search intent for Turkey to United Kingdom (TR→UK) moves from Mersin toward Northern England and the North West while keeping brand signals accurate.

Who to contact for Turkey-origin exports

For Turkey-origin freight, removals and customs programmes toward the UK and Europe, use the Han Bros Cargo ecosystem on hanbroscargo.com—distinct from the UK-domain UK→TR messaging on hanbroscargo.co.uk.

Planning checklist (TR→UK)

  • Commercial invoice, packing list and harmonised codes ready before pickup.
  • UK EORI and importer of record clarity for commercial lines; residence proof for personal effects.
  • Insurance valuation aligned to CMR or marine policy terms, depending on mode.
  • Restricted and dual-use items screened early—UK Border Targeting operates strict controls.

Typical modes

Most Turkey→UK programmes combine domestic positioning, an ocean main leg to Felixstowe, Southampton or Liverpool ranges, then UK domestic delivery. Time-critical cargo may split to air via IST/ESB/ADB gateways into LHR/STN/MAN—subject to airline product and DG rules.

Local positioning note

Mersin is a primary deep-sea gateway for southern Turkey—useful for FCL and heavy pallets.
